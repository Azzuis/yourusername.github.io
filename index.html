About Us

Two guys from Victoria, BC building technology that stays out of your way.

Adam Geggie-Bowker

Co-Founder · Lead Designer

Victoria-raised hardware builder and autodidact. Studied electrical engineering at UVic and spent a formative co-op at Gregory C. Marshall Naval Architects as a systems designer — picking up AutoCAD, Rhino, and a deep appreciation for how things are actually built. Brings a rare mix of electronics, programming, and self-taught mechanical intuition. Driven by a simple goal: use technology to make people's lives genuinely easier. Not flashier, not louder — just a little bit better. That belief is the thread running through everything Remindr builds.

Dawsen Foreman

Co-Founder · Business

Fresh out of UVic's Gustavson School of Business, Dawsen is the operational backbone of Remindr. While Adam builds, Dawsen handles the side of the company that keeps it alive — strategy, partnerships, and turning a good idea into a real business. Shares the same core belief: that the best technology is the kind you barely notice.
